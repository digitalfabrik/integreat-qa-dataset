## Answer: Sie können sich an die Fachstelle zur Vermeidung von Wohnungslosigkeit wenden. ## Sentence numbers: 2, 13

(Sentence number 2 clearly states that you can contact the Fachstelle zur Vermeidung von Wohnungslosigkeit if you are in danger of losing your home. Sentence number 13 explains that you need to make an appointment with the responsible social worker at the Sozialbürgerhaus for a consultation, but it does not negate the fact that the Fachstelle is the entity that can provide help.)
