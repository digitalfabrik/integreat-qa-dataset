## Answer: You have the option of taking statutory or private health insurance. Most people have statutory health insurance - unless they earn a lot at work and exceed a certain amount. When you work, health insurance is paid by you (the employee) and the employer. In order to qualify for private health insurance, a person must meet certain criteria (e.g. a predefined gross income threshold). You take out health insurance with a health insurance company. There are many different health insurance providers in Germany. Every person has the right to choose their health insurer.
