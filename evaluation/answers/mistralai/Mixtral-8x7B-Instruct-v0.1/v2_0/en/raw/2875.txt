## Answer: The requirements for training with tolerated status include a training contract, submission of the application with necessary documents to the Foreigners' Registration Office, qualified training in a state-recognized or comparably regulated training occupation with a standard training period of at least two years, entry of the vocational training in the register of vocational training relationships, clarified identity, and not receiving a training permit with an introductory qualification (EQ). Additionally, tolerated persons who have already started training during the asylum procedure or those who want to start training and have been in possession of a tolerated stay permit for three months are also eligible. ## Sentence numbers: 4-7, 9-13, 15, 17-18
