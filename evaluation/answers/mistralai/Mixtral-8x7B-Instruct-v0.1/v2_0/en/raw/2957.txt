## Answer: Freedom of expression in Germany is the right of all people to form their own opinions, express them freely, and disseminate them publicly. This includes the freedom of the media and protection of personal honor and dignity of others. It also allows for criticism of the government and religion, as well as the creation of provocative artistic works. However, it does not permit the use of anti-constitutional symbols, insulting expressions, defamation or slander, or incitement of hatred and violence. ## Sentence numbers: 0-11, 13-16
