## Answer: Citizens of a third country require a permit for permanent residence in Germany, which includes access to the labor market. Asylum seekers in ongoing proceedings are not allowed to work for nine months, but can apply for a work permit after nine months if their application has been accepted by the BAMF or if they come from a safe country of origin. Tolerated persons can apply for a work permit after six months of toleration if they come from a safe country of origin, except for those with a tolerated stay permit due to unclear identity. ## Sentence numbers: 4, 20-22, 30-33, 44-47, 53-56, 59
