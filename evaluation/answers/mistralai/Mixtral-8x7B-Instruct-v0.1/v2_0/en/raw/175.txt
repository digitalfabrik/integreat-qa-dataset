## Answer: There are five kinds of residence documents. ## Sentence numbers: 5-6, 11-12, 21-22, 24-25, 34-35

The five kinds of residence documents are proof of arrival for asylum seekers, residence permit for asylum seekers, fictitious certificate for recognized refugees, residence permit for recognized refugees, and acquiescence for tolerated individuals. Each document has different conditions, validity periods, and responsibilities for job placement and social benefits.
