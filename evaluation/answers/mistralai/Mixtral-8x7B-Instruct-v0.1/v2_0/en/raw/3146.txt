## Answer: Technical colleges are suitable for people who have completed vocational training or have professional experience. The duration of study is up to two years full-time or up to 4 years part-time, and the degrees offered are the state final examination or master craftsman examination, secondary school leaving certificate, general higher education entrance qualification, or Fachhochschulreife (after a supplementary examination).
