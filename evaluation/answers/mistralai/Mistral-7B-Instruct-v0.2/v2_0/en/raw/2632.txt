## Answer: To register your child for school in Germany, you must wait until after 6 months of residence, then ask at the town hall which school your child can go to, and bring your identity card, birth certificate or proof of arrival, and all documents relating to your child to the registration. The registration dates vary by school type and location, so it's important to ask the school or town hall for specific information.
