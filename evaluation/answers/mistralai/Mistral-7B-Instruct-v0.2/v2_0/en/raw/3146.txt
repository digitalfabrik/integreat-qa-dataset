Answer: To attend a technical college, one needs to have completed vocational training or have professional experience. The duration of the technical college can be full-time for up to two years or part-time for up to 4 years. The requirements for entering a technical college include a secondary school leaving certificate, general higher education entrance qualification, or Fachhochschulreife (after a supplementary examination).
