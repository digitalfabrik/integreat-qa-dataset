## Answer: The following conditions must be met to issue a settlement permit: legal residence in Germany for at least five years, a valid residence permit that was not issued for a temporary purpose or for humanitarian reasons, proof of secure livelihood and living space, proof of sufficient pension provision, and sufficient German language skills and basic knowledge of the legal and social system and living conditions in Germany.
